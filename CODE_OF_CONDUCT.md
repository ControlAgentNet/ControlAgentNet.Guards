# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in ControlAgentNet a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**

- Being respectful, constructive, and practical in all interactions
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Giving and gracefully accepting feedback focused on the work
- Acknowledging mistakes and learning from them
- Prioritizing what is best for the community and the project

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments of any kind
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at `security@controlagentnet.ai`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
